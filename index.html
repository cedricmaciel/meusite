Games

BugFront

◀
▶

BugFront is a fun and challenging game where different types of insects descend from the top of the screen, and you must stop them from reaching you. You start with 5 cherries, which represent your defensive energy. Each time an insect crosses your line, you lose one cherry. When all cherries are gone, the game is over.

Azure Rider

◀
▶

Get ready for an intense arcade space shooter! Azure Rider brings fast action, heavy-metal energy, and three explosive stages filled with enemies, challenges, and boss battles. Pilot your starship through space, destroy waves of enemy ships, and stay alert — speed is everything. Each phase introduces new obstacles and faster enemy formations that will test your reflexes.
